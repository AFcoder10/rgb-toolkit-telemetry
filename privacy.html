Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

4 Zone RGB Toolkit respects your privacy. Our telemetry system is designed to be completely transparent and minimally invasive.

What Data We Collect

  • PC Name: We only collect the name of your computer.
  • Active Ping: We use a simple background ping to know when you are currently using the app.

Why We Collect It

This data is strictly used to display who and how many users are actively using the application on this dashboard. No IP addresses, hardware identifiers, typing data, or usage behaviors are ever tracked or stored.
